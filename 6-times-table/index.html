About This 6 Times Table Practice

What is 6 times table practice?

This page provides focused 6 times table practice to help students learn and memorize multiplication facts that include 6. Each question uses the 6 times table so learners can improve recall through repeated practice.

Key facts and patterns for the 6 times table

  • Multiples of 6: 6, 12, 18, 24, 30, 36, 42, 48, 54, 60, 66, 72
  • Helpful facts: 6 × 4 = 24, 6 × 6 = 36, 6 × 8 = 48
  • Pattern: 6 × n = (5 × n) + n (use the 5s, then add one more set).

Who should practice the 6 times table?

This practice is helpful for elementary students learning multiplication, as well as anyone who wants extra review of the 6 times table. Parents and teachers can use it for quick drills, warmups, or homework help.

How do I get better at the 6 times table?

Practice a little each day and focus on accuracy first. When you miss a question, take a moment to learn the correct fact and try again. Building a streak can help you stay motivated while you practice the 6 times table.

Is this 6 times table practice free?

Yes. This is a free online 6 times table practice tool that runs in your browser. Your best streak is saved locally on your device.
